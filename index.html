Favourite Sneakers

Air Jordan 1

Overview of Sneaker

The Air Jordan 1 was originally released in 1985 It was a shoe made for the 1984 #3 Draft Pick, Michael Jordan. The shoes were originally priced at $64.99 but as of today with all the collabs with the brand and the specific shoe model, there are Air Jordan 1s reaching prices of over $11,000.

Air Force 1

Overview of Sneaker

The Air Force 1 is a classic sneaker and silouhette that was created by Bruce Kilgore and introduced in 1982. These shoes started off at a price of $89.95 and now retail for about 120CAD. This is another sneaker that has multiple collaborations with various brands, resulting in high resell prices.
